About Us

Unwise Urge is a Rush tribute band based in Pittsburgh, PA. The project was born out of a lifelong love of the music of Rush and the desire to recreate it to the most exacting standards possible. We understand that any band's sound is as much a part of the chemical (some may say chimerical!) makeup of its members, and while nobody could possibly recreate the exact sound of Rush (which is in the heads, hearts, and hands of its members), we strive to come as close as humanly possible to capturing those magical moments that inspired us as young musicians all those many years ago.

The musical focus of Unwise Urge is the first "golden era" of Rush - the 1980s - although we include many songs from the 1970s through present that embody the musical spirit of the band that continues to serve as inspiration for musicians young and old around the world. As the members of Rush would tell you, creating music is a constant process of evolution and reinvention, and this drives our sense of purpose in Unwise Urge. We know that Rush will not record and tour forever, and with that in mind, our guiding principle is simply "would our work make the real Rush proud?" This drives us to prepare and perform with the same exacting standards that everyone has come to expect from their favorite band.

While we take our music seriously (as it deserves to be), we also love to share our joy of recreating Rush's music with our audiences. Just as Rush has done throughout its career, we make our live performances enjoyable and inclusive of our audience (after all, none of us - audience included - would be here without Rush in the first place!). We want to make the concert experience as close as possible to the real thing, and we include and continually add to all of those exciting show elements that have become staples of an actual Rush concert.

Join us as we continue to honor and celebrate over four decades of visionary music and performance from our favorite band, Rush.
